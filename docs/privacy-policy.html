Firewall Analyzer - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Firewall Analyzer is an offline desktop application that analyzes firewall configuration files. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the application to operate entirely on your device with no data collection of any kind.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Firewall Analyzer:

  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers
  • Does not include analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting
  • Does not track usage patterns or behavior
  • Does not use cookies or any form of persistent tracking

File Processing

When you import a firewall configuration file, the file is read and processed entirely on your local device. The file contents are held in memory only for the duration of the analysis session and are never written to disk, transmitted over the network, or stored in any persistent form beyond what you explicitly export.

Exported Data

The application allows you to export analysis results as a JSON file. This export is saved to a location you choose on your device. We have no access to these exported files.

Third-Party Services

Firewall Analyzer does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

The application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@bardsec.com.
